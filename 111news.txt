Man taken to hospital after suffering self-inflicted injuries
QUEENSBURY | Police surrounded a house on Caroline Street for more 
than an hour Wednesday after a report that a man inside the home had threatened 
to harm himself. 

The man was removed from the house shortly after 1 p.m., having suffered 
self-inflicted injuries, police said. He was taken to Glens Falls Hospital.

Three or four houses on Caroline Street were evacuated and part of the street 
closed as a result of the incident. Officers from the Warren County Sheriff's 
Office and State Police responded.

A heavy police presence, including officers with assault weapons, was evident 
at 1 p.m. 

Caroline Street is located south of Main Street in Queensbury.
