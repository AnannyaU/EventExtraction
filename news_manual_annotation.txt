5	`` The assumption is that he 's the all-time youngest , '' American River College spokesman Scott Crow told NBC Bay Area on Thursday .
6	`` But we do n't have all the archives to completely confirm .
7	He was definitely the youngest this year . ''

9	Abraham , who joined MENSA when he was only four years old , was home-schooled after feeling `` bored '' in his classes , according to NBC Bay Area .
10	He graduated high school last June at age 10 , after completing California 's early-exit high school exam .
11	He even received a congratulatory message from President Barack Obama and several California officials .
12	His mother , Taji Abraham , said he has always been ahead of the class .

14	Tanishq Abraham said some of the students at the college `` were intimidated '' by him but a lot of others `` were really happy '' to have a kid in their classes .
